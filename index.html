Our First Trip to New York City!

In May of 2015, our family made it's first visit to the Big Apple.
Below are some highlights of our trip, including pictures and links to learn more about the more interesting sites.

The Sites
  • Ed Sullivan Theater

    • We arrived in NYC on May 21, 2015 ... the day after David Letterman's final Late Show aired.
    When we passed the famous theater, they still had the David Letterman signage up, so we caught a piece of history!

The Food
  • Bar Americain

    • Our favorite chef to watch on Food Network is Bobby Flay, so the first place we ate had to be at one of his restaurants. Bar Americain was right on our way, walking from our hotel in mid-town to the play we were going to see in the Theater District. We actually planned the timing of our trip so we could see Jim Parsons (Sheldon in Big Bang Theory) in the off-Broadway production, "An Act of God".
